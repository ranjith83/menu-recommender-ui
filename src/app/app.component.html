Our Philosophy

Hotel Web App is a starred restaurant located in Donegal's West End. Our food comes from the specific place that is the west of Ireland. We hope to reveal the distinct and various foodstuffs that make up our particular landscape, through our farms, the wildlands and the shores that surround us.

The natural course of the seasons dictates our ever-changing and evolving menu. Our tasting menu is an immersive dining experience of Irish food through the ages with a contemporary filter.
